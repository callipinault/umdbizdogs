About Bizdogs

The BizDogs Living and Learning Community is a group of first-year, pre-business students within the Labovitz School of Business and Economics (LSBE), who elect to live together in a traditional residence hall community and take classes together, creating an environment where community members can thrive socially and academically.

Visit our LSBE page

Want to join?

Students will be admitted on a first-come, first-served basis, so get your application in early. There is no additional cost to participate in the BizDogs program.

Visit our LSBE page
Community service projects

The BizDogs are involved in numerous projects aimed at bettering the community. Join us, and you’ll get a chance to participate in some of these and help improve things for those around you!

Social gatherings

We host a multitude of social events, including study sessions and BizDogs lunch with LSBE faculty right here on campus! They are a great way to meet new people and develop lasting friendships with fellow students.
